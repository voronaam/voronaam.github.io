Josie Osborne is a marine biologist currently serving her third term as mayor of Tofino. Osborne has lived in Tofino for more than 20 years, first moving to the area to work as a fisheries biologist for the Nuu-chah-nulth Tribal Council. She has served as Chair of the Board for the Island Coastal Economic Trust, Chair of Tourism Tofino and Executive Director of both the Tofino Botanical Gardens Foundation and the Raincoast Education Society.
